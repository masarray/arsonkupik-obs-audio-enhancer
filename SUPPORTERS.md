## Recognition requests

A supporter who wants to be listed may open a non-sensitive repository issue after the contribution is complete and provide:

- preferred display name;
- optional organization name;
- optional safe public link;
- requested recognition group;
- confirmation that the information may be published.

Do not post transaction screenshots, payment references, personal addresses, phone numbers, bank details, or other private financial information.

The project may verify a recognition request privately before publishing it. Listings may be edited or removed on request.

See [Funding policy](docs/FUNDING_POLICY.md) for the complete rules.
